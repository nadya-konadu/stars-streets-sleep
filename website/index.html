Overview

Exploring how city-level nighttime light trends relate to sentiment and content in dreams in Toronto & Mississauga, 2024–2025.

Design Rationale

I chose charts that let users explore light intensity and dream emotion patterns side-by-side, so it’s easy to see how they might relate.
